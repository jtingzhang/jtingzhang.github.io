Bio

I am a Ph.D. student in the department of Computer Science at Purdue University. I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Aniket Kate at FreedomLab. Previously, I graduated from Sun Yat-sen University, working with Prof. Wuhui Chen and Prof. Zibin Zheng.

I am particularly interested in blockchain, distributed system and protocol security. My goal is to bridge the gap between academic and industrial research on blockchains.

In addition to doing research, I also enjoy singing. My friends call me Purdue's Eason Chan. My famous song - Half Moon Serenade, however, is created by another Cantonese singer Hacken Lee.
