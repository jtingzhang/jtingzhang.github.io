Bio

Welcome to Jianting Zhang's homepage. I am a first-year Ph.D. student of Computer Science at Purdue University. I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Aniket Kate at FreedomLab. Previously, I graduated from Sun Yat-sen University, working with Prof. Wuhui Chen and Prof. Zibin Zheng.

I am interested in distributed system, blockchain scalability, and smart contracts. My goal is to develop a blockchain system which not only supports thousands of transaction throughput but also provide security for smart contract execution.
